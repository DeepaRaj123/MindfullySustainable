Services for Educations Sector

It’s our moral responsibility to educate our children about the importance of being sustainable. By teaching our children to adopt the sustainable practices at an early childhood and throughout their journey we can help the nation to get the responsible sustainable citizens of tomorrow.

The educational institutes have a major role to play to save the planet earth as they can promote the concept of sustainability to a wider community.

We encourage and offer various services to the educational institutes to promote and implement the sustainable practices among the students, their parents and to the extended family. Adaptation of these practices not only save the environment it improves the overall wellbeing of our future generation.

What we Offer

Our services are specifically designed to help you:

We analyze the system and mode of teaching of various institutes from sustainability perspective.

We help the institute to adopt an ambitious yet achievable plan.

We help in the recycling process and in using recyclable materials wherever possible.

Work on the minimum use of natural resources, eg: several proposals to save water and electricity.

Help the children to follow the practice of recycling even at their home and in a wider community.

Educating the children about natural decomposition cycle and recycling the water in gardening etc.

We help the children to understand the importance of plants and taking care of them.

Imbibe the value of respect for the environment and wildlife.

We help the institute to plan several excursion and incursion plans to promote the idea of sustainability among the children.

We help the institute to adopt plan to serve healthy organic foods among the children.

A plan to minimize the food waste & use of paper.

Regular auditing plans.
